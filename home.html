Our goal is to return farmer-owned water content sensor data back to farmers through existing monitoring networks using informative metrics and easy to access tools. The USU Environmental Soil Physics Group.




USU Environmental Soil Physics Group

http://soilphysics.usu.edu


  • For farmland within sight (line-of-sight up to 12 miles) of a Utah Climate Center weather station, radio telemetry can be used to transmit on-farm water content data to an online data storage and analysis facility.

  • Uploaded data can be accessed by smart phone or computer to assess the soil water status in support of more efficient irrigation management.
